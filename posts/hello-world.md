# Hello World — Welcome to My Blog

Welcome to my corner of the internet! I'm excited to finally launch this blog after months of planning and building.

## What to Expect

This blog will be a place where I share:

- **Technical tutorials** on Next.js, React, and TypeScript
- **Design insights** from building real-world projects
- **Lessons learned** from shipping products at scale
- **Tools and workflows** that make me more productive

## Why I Started Writing

I've been building web applications for over 5 years, and I've learned so much from the community. It's time to give back. Writing helps me think more clearly, and I hope these posts help you too.

## The Stack

This site itself is built with:

- **Next.js 14** with the App Router
- **Tailwind CSS** for styling
- **TypeScript** for type safety
- **Markdown** for blog content

Stay tuned for more posts. This is just the beginning!
